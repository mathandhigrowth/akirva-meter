Information We Collect

Akirva Meter is designed to collect only the information necessary for its core functionality.

  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymous app usage information may be collected to improve performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

Akirva Meter does not store or upload your trip location data to external servers. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
