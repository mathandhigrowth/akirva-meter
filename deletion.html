Account Deletion Request

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Akirva Meter respects your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Akirva Meter account, please send an email using the instructions below:

  • Email Address: dhigrowthofficial@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: Account Deletion Request
  • Email Body: Please include your registered email address or any relevant details that help us identify your account.
Note: For security reasons, account deletion requests must be sent from the email address associated with your Akirva Meter account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User account information (if applicable)
  • Stored preferences and settings
  • Any saved trip or fare history associated with your account

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or legal compliance). Any retained data will be securely stored and deleted once no longer required.
